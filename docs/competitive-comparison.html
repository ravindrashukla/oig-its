OIG-ITS Unique Advantages

🤖 22 AI Algorithms

More AI capabilities than any competitor. Fraud ring detection, evidence scoring, predictive analytics, natural language search — no other platform offers this depth.

🏛️ Purpose-Built for OIG

Not a generic CRM adapted for investigations. Every feature designed for OIG-specific workflows: subpoena packages, chain of custody, Title 5 pay calculations.

💰 No Per-User Licensing

Competitors charge $50-300/user/month. OIG-ITS has zero per-user fees. For 30 users: competitors cost $18K-108K/year vs $0 licensing for OIG-ITS.

📞 Public Intake with AI

Only platform with built-in public hotline + whistleblower portal with AI risk scoring, complaint deduplication, and automatic priority escalation.

🔒 Full Source Code

Complete source code ownership. No vendor lock-in. Customize any feature without contractor dependency. Deploy on-premise or in government cloud.

📊 Integrated Modules

Training, timesheets, inventory, calendar built-in. Competitors require separate products at additional cost. One system for everything.
